Notes: 2012 is leap year (Feb 29 included). Holidays shown are the commonly observed regular non-working holidays in the Philippines for reference.

New Year's Day — Jan 1

New Year’s Day — regular holiday marking the first day of the Gregorian year. Traditions include family gatherings and fireworks.
Maundy Thursday — Apr 5

Maundy Thursday — regular holiday (part of Holy Week observance).

Good Friday — Apr 6

Good Friday — regular holiday commemorating the crucifixion of Jesus Christ.

Araw ng Kagitingan — Apr 9

Araw ng Kagitingan (Day of Valor) — regular holiday honoring those who fought in Bataan during WWII.

Labor Day — May 1

Labor Day — regular holiday honoring workers and labor movements.

Independence Day — Jun 12

Independence Day — regular holiday commemorating the declaration of independence on June 12, 1898.

National Heroes Day — Aug 27

National Heroes Day — regular holiday observed on the last Monday of August to honor Filipino heroes.

Bonifacio Day — Nov 30

Bonifacio Day — regular holiday honoring Andrés Bonifacio, a revolutionary leader.

Christmas Day — Dec 25

Christmas Day — regular holiday celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ.

Rizal Day — Dec 30

Rizal Day — regular holiday commemorating the execution of Dr. José Rizal, national hero.
